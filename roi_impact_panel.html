Impact & ROI

The value of this pipeline is measured on defensible ground — time saved, travel avoided, and capacity freed — not a single headline sales-lift number. Every figure below is live and inspectable; adjust the assumptions and watch the model recompute.

Why not a sales-lift %?

Conversion (quote → order) can't be tracked: the CRM creates one opportunity per store, so a won 150-store bid records as ~75 "yes" · 0 "no". A rate needs a denominator, and the losses were never captured — so any lift % would be fabricated. We lead with what we can defend.
